Capstone Project

Parkbai: Web

The Parkbai website was developed for our parking lot owner clients to automate the monitoring of their parking areas and eliminate manual processes such as parking fee transactions. The platform integrates digital payments through PayPal for convenient driver payments. Additionally, we implemented a central database and Internet of Thing for toll gate automation using RFID scanners and sensors for every parking space, enabling real-time monitoring.

Parkbai: Mobile

The Parkbai mobile application is designed for drivers to connect with our parking system, making it easier to search for parking lots associated with our network. Through the Parkbai app, we offer real-time parking slot availability, secure online payments, and balance checks. Additionally, drivers can view real-time map locations, ensuring seamless navigation to available spots. The app also provides detailed parking history for every transaction made using the system.

Parkbai: Internet of Things

Parkbai integrates technology to automate manual transactions, eliminating the need for cash payments, parking tickets, and manual operation of parking toll gates. We design a parking sensor device to allow for real-time monitoring for parking lot owners to ensure enhanced safety and security for both customers and staff. We also provide RFID cards to drivers, enabling secure, cashless, and legitimate transactions with real-time tracking.
